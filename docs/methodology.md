# Methodology

## Objective

To evaluate discrepancies between platform-reported conversions and GA4 data.

---

## Approach

### Step 1: Data Preparation

* Collected data from GA4, Google Ads, and Meta Ads
* Standardised campaign naming
* Cleaned inconsistencies

---

### Step 2: Data Modelling

* Created a star schema
* Built dimension tables for campaign, date, channel, device & country

---

### Step 3: Metric Development

* Defined GA4 performance metrics
* Calculated platform conversions
* Built comparison metrics (Gap, Gap %)

---

### Step 4: Analysis

* Compared conversions at campaign level
* Identified discrepancies
* Segmented campaigns by reliability

---

### Step 5: Insights

* Identified over-reporting trends
* Evaluated impact on decision-making

---

## Key Assumption

GA4 is used as a proxy for actual performance.

---

## Limitations

* Attribution differences cannot be fully eliminated
* Simulated dataset
* No user-level tracking
